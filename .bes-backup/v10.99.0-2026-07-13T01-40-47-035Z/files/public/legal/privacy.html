Privacy Policy

Brian English Studio V1.0 is a teaching workflow app for English teachers.

Data stored in the app

The app may store teacher-generated content, saved prompts, question banks, AI settings, theme and language preferences in the browser. When Supabase Auth is configured, account email, name, school, role and approval status are stored in Supabase.

AI keys

If users enter their own AI API keys, the current V1 app stores those keys in the browser for BYOK usage. Do not enter keys on shared devices. A backend proxy is recommended for a later production hardening release.

Third-party tools

Game Hub opens third-party websites such as Games4ESL, Wordwall, Educaplay and similar platforms. Their own privacy policies apply when opened or embedded.

Contact

For support, contact the app owner or administrator.
