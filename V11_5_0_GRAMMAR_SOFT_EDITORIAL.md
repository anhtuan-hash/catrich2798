# Brian English Studio V11.5.0 — Grammar Builder Soft Editorial

## Design language

Grammar Builder now uses the **Soft Editorial Cards** design system:

- warm white cards on a calm academic canvas;
- 1px borders instead of heavy black outlines;
- large rounded corners and restrained shadows;
- muted blue, green, purple and terracotta accents;
- selected states use a light tint, left accent rail and clear check indicator;
- no full-card vivid gradients;
- two setup cards per row on desktop;
- large readable typography across setup, editor, audit, publish and modal views.

## Functional scope retained

- 7-step production workflow and 9 functional cards;
- 100 grouped grammar domains and custom focus request;
- Brian AI Gateway, batch generation and Diagnostic Engine;
- Content Editor, Quality Audit, Teacher Vault and Item Bank;
- Connected Workflow to eight Brian applications;
- 12/12 Vercel Hobby functions and no SQL migration.
