# V11.3.1 — English Global Success Lesson Architect

## Scope
Lesson Architect now defaults to Grade 11 English, Global Success, school year 2025–2026.

## Output language
Every generated lesson plan, worksheet, activity instruction and answer key is in English. Vietnamese is permitted only in proper names and official document numbers.

## Digital competence
The lesson engine uses the six-domain, 24-component learner framework from Circular No. 02/2025/TT-BGDĐT:
1. Data and information literacy
2. Communication and collaboration in digital environments
3. Digital content creation
4. Safety
5. Problem solving
6. Artificial intelligence application

Each lesson selects only 1–3 relevant component competences and must show:
- code and English name
- lesson-specific performance indicator
- tool/resource
- observable evidence or product
- safety, privacy, copyright or AI-ethics condition where relevant

Digital competence must appear in learner actions and products, not only in the Objectives section.

## Global Success pedagogy
The generator adapts to Getting Started, Language, Reading, Speaking, Listening, Writing, Communication and Culture/CLIL, Looking Back and Project, Reviews and Tests.
